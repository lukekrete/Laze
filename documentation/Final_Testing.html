Test Strategy and Plan for LAZE

LAZE will be developed and tested by the CP312 class of 2018 under the supervision of Dr David Brown.

The strategy is to use testing techniques in all phases of project development life cycle. Verification and Validation techniques, unit testing, integration testing, non-functional testing, end user testing and some exploratory testing.

  • Requirements and Analysis SQ - Team determined testability, ambiguity, validity and verification
  • GUI Prototype Validation SQA - Team ensured that the prototype best reflected user requirements
  • SPMP SQA - Overall QA Cycle
    • Included any grammar check, content & date updates and overall aesthetics
  • Design SQA - Includes design review & validation
    • ensuring all UML diagrams are up to date with classes, methods
  • Testing- Unit, Functional, Integration, Performance, Security, Browser, OS
  • Test Cases- Detailed test cases will be designed and executed as per the timeline
  • Defects will be reported to design and development team where applicable.
  • Bug fixes will be part of bi weekly development release cycle, Sanity check followed by functional and regression testing including bug verification.
